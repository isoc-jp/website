Dear ISOC-JP Members,

We hereby announce the results of the 2025 ISOC-JP Board Election. This year, the number of positions up for election was three, and the Nomination Committee selected three candidates.
As stated in the ‘Notice of Board Election’, ‘When the number of candidates does not exceed the number of positions up for election, the candidates shall be declared elected without a vote.’
Accordingly, a report was made at the ISOC Japan Chapter 2025 Annual General Meeting held on Thursday, 18 December 2025, confirming the following individuals as the next officers.

Katsuyuki Hasebe \
Kumi Mitani \
Yoshinobu Matsuzaki

Pursuant to Article IV, Section 3 of the Articles of Association, the term of office for the elected officers shall be two years.


Sincerely, \
Miya Kohno \
ISOC-JP 2025 Voting Administrator
